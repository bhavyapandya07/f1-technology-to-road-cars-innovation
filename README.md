## ğŸ“š Key Case Studies

This research highlights real-world examples where **Formula 1 technology successfully influenced production vehicles**:

- **McLaren MP4/1**  
  Introduced the first full **carbon-fiber composite monocoque** in Formula 1, redefining safety and lightweight design in motorsport and beyond.

- **Ferrari Semi-Automatic Gearbox**  
  Originally developed for Formula 1, paddle-shift transmissions later became standard in high-performance and consumer sports cars.

- **KERS (Kinetic Energy Recovery System)**  
  A breakthrough in energy recovery that directly influenced modern hybrid systems and regenerative braking technologies.

- **Mercedes-AMG ONE**  
  A road-legal hypercar using **direct Formula 1 hybrid powertrain technology**, demonstrating the closest link between F1 engineering and production vehicles.

These examples show how racing innovation improves **efficiency, performance, and safety** for everyday road cars.
